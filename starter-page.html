About Us

Dowealth Training and Consulting is a forward-thinking corporate capacity-building and recruiting firm based in Lagos, Nigeria. We help companies build a network of highly skilled professionals to achieve their goals.

Our consultants maintain a high project completion rate of 97%, with only 3% affected by unforeseen challenges such as project shutdowns or health-related issues.

We specialize in soft skills training, capacity-building workshops, retreats, and full-cycle recruitment services, ensuring mutual growth for both our clients and candidates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Dowealth Training and Consulting.

1. What services does Dowealth Training and Consulting offer?

We offer corporate training, workforce outsourcing, recruitment and selection, and HR consulting.

2. How do you customize your corporate training programs?

Our programs are tailored to your organization's needs, focusing on skills gaps, objectives, and desired outcomes.

3. What industries do you work with?

We serve various industries, including finance, healthcare, technology, manufacturing, and retail.

4. How can Dowealth assist with recruitment and selection?

We streamline hiring through job market analysis, screening, interviews, and background checks.

5. What is the process for workforce outsourcing?

We source, vet, and manage staff based on your needs, handling payroll, benefits, and compliance.

6. Can you help us with HR policies and compliance?

Yes, we design HR policies and ensure compliance with labor laws, offering employee relations support.

7. How do you ensure the quality of your training programs?

Our programs are delivered by experts, updated regularly, and evaluated through feedback and assessments.

8. What types of HR consulting services do you provide?

  • Strategic workforce planning
  • Employee engagement strategies
  • Leadership development
  • Organization design
  • HR audits and assessments

9. What makes Dowealth different from other firms?

We offer personalized solutions, industry expertise, and a proven track record of measurable results.

10. How can we get started with your services?

Contact us via phone or email to schedule a consultation and discuss your specific needs.

11. Do you offer virtual training sessions?

Yes, we provide interactive virtual training sessions designed to achieve effective outcomes.

12. How do you measure the success of training programs?

We use feedback surveys, assessments, and performance metrics to evaluate training effectiveness.

CEO, Dowealth Training and Consulting

As the CEO of Dowealth Training and Consulting, it is my honour to extend a warm welcome to you. We are a dynamic and forward-thinking firm committed to empowering organisations and individuals through comprehensive solutions in corporate training, workforce outsourcing, recruitment and selection, education, and HR consulting.

At Dowealth Training and Consulting, we believe that the true strength of any business lies in the capabilities of its people. Our mission is to help organizations unlock the full potential of their workforce by providing tailored training programs that foster growth, innovation, and leadership. We understand the challenges faced by businesses today, and our solutions are designed to address the unique needs of each client, ensuring a lasting impact on productivity and success.

Our recruitment and selection services are rooted in a deep understanding of talent and organizational fit. We take pride in connecting businesses with top-tier professionals who align with their culture and long-term objectives. Our team is dedicated to sourcing, evaluating, and securing the best talent, so you can focus on driving your business forward.

Additionally, our workforce outsourcing solutions provide organizations with the flexibility and efficiency needed to scale operations while reducing overhead costs. Whether you need short-term support or long-term workforce management, we are here to provide expert guidance and seamless solutions.

We are passionate about fostering continuous learning and development, and our education services are tailored to support both individuals and organizations in their pursuit of excellence. Through personalized education pathways, we help people grow, thrive, and achieve their career aspirations.

At Dowealth, we also offer expert HR consulting services to guide your organization through the complexities of people management. Our team of seasoned consultants is here to help you create effective HR strategies that promote engagement, retention, and organizational growth.

Thank you for considering Dowealth Training and Consulting as your partner. We are excited to work with you, providing exceptional services that lead to real, measurable results. Let us help you build a future of success, innovation, and excellence.
